# iSchoolVerse – Professional Lovable Prompts

A comprehensive suite of **5 production-ready prompts** for building a world-class educational platform.

---

## 📚 The 5 Modules

### 🔐 **PROMPT 1: Unified Identity, Onboarding & Master Dashboard**
Build the master authentication system with role-based dashboards (Student, Teacher, Admin). Every user gets a unique **iSchoolVerse ID** (ISV-ROLE-XXXXXX). Dashboard shows personalized widgets: today's timetable, assignments, performance, attendance.

**Time Estimate**: 2-3 weeks

---

### 📅 **PROMPT 2: iSchool Timetables & Calendar (Smart Scheduling System)**
Build an intelligent timetable generator that auto-creates conflict-free weekly schedules using constraint-satisfaction algorithms. Includes drag-and-drop manual adjustments, live conflict detection, and a unified calendar system supporting lessons, assignments, exams, and meetings.

**Time Estimate**: 3-4 weeks

---

### 📖 **PROMPT 3: iSchoolBook & iSchool Notes (Content & Learning Workspace)**
Build a digital content engine (Subject → Topic → Subtopic hierarchy) with rich media support (YouTube, PhET simulations, 3D models). Pair it with an interactive learner workspace where students annotate, highlight, take notes, and complete inline quizzes. Features split-screen side-by-side view and cross-subject integration activities.

**Time Estimate**: 3-4 weeks

---

### 📊 **PROMPT 4: iSchool Monitor & iSchool Reports (Analytics & Academic Intelligence)**
Build teaching analytics that auto-track attendance (from whiteboard sessions), engagement metrics, and lesson delivery rates. Pair it with academic reporting: teachers enter scores, system auto-calculates grades and ranks, generates professional report cards (PDF) with trends and remarks.

**Time Estimate**: 2-3 weeks

---

### 🖥️ **PROMPT 5: iSchool Whiteboard (Live Online Classroom)**
Build a professional live classroom engine comparable to ClassIn or BigBlueButton. Features: multi-participant video, digital whiteboard with drawing tools, screen sharing, iSchoolBook content integration, PhET simulation embedding, live quizzes with real-time results, chat, breakout rooms, full session recording + synchronized playback, and attendance auto-logging.

**Time Estimate**: 4-6 weeks

---

## 🎯 Recommended Build Order

| Step | Module | Why | Timeline |
|------|--------|-----|----------|
| **1** | Identity & Dashboard | Foundation — all modules connect here | Weeks 1-3 |
| **2** | Timetables & Calendar | Structure — drives scheduling for all | Weeks 4-7 |
| **3** | iSchoolBook & Notes | Content layer — feeds whiteboard and reports | Weeks 8-11 |
| **4** | Monitor & Reports | Data layer — needs lessons and engagement | Weeks 12-14 |
| **5** | Whiteboard | Capstone — integrates everything live | Weeks 15-20 |

**Total**: ~20 weeks (full-stack team, agile sprints)

## 🔗 How They Connect

```
Dashboard (1) 
├── shows today's lessons from Timetable (2)
├── displays assignments from Calendar (2)
├── links to iSchoolBook content (3)
└── shows grades from Reports (4)

Timetable (2)
├── auto-syncs lessons to Calendar (2)
├── feeds lesson context to Whiteboard (5)
└── provides structure for Monitor (4)

iSchoolBook (3)
├── can be opened inside Whiteboard (5)
└── links to student Notes for each topic (3)

Whiteboard (5)
├── auto-records attendance to Monitor (4)
├── records session for Notes (3)
└── exports board snapshots to Notes (3)

Monitor (4) 
└── feeds analytics to Reports (4) & Dashboard (1)

Reports (4)
└── displays results on Student Dashboard (1)
```
